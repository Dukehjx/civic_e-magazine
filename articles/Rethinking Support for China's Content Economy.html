Rethinking Support for China's Content Economy 重新思考对中国内容经济的支持政策

Digital Culture • January 2026 数字文化 • 2026年1月
Summary: Evaluating the effectiveness of current policies supporting high-quality internet content creation and providing optimization suggestions.

I. Primary Issues

1. Policy Implementation Misalignment: Selection Criteria Mismatch with Content Creator Characteristics

Through field research with content creators, the research team found significant misalignment in the implementation of "Lang Nine Provisions" in Yangpu and Huangpu concentration zones. First, policy support target selection criteria do not match content creator characteristics. Content creators are mostly individuals or small teams with small, agile, and flexible characteristics, typically operating under individual flexible employment or registered as individual businesses for operations and social security contributions. However, Yangpu explicitly requires that all UP hosts and content creation teams must register companies in the district to enjoy "Lang Nine Provisions" support policies, entering as company entities.

Second, policy support content does not match content creator needs. Currently, Yangpu and Huangpu stipulate they can provide free office space and Shanghai hukou benefits for UP hosts meeting support standards. However, many UP hosts reported that content creators need to move through the city to shoot material, with extremely low dependence on office space. Moreover, many UP hosts are from Shanghai itself, and their demand for Shanghai hukou is not as urgent as other types of talent.

2. Policy Implementation Overreach: Hidden Thresholds Exist for Quality Content Project Applications

In September 2025, the "Shanghai Internet Quality Content Creation Project Application Guide" was released, focusing on supporting high-quality brand activities, quality content creation, and quality content going global projects. However, many content creators reported that hidden thresholds exist for this project application, namely that if applicants are not registered in Yangpu or Huangpu districts, their submitted content projects will not be accepted. Additionally, Yangpu and Huangpu have not formed cross-regional coordination and differentiated competition in the quality content creation field, with increasingly fierce competition between the two districts for internet content creator resources.

3. Policy Implementation Gaps: Core Functions Missing in V-Collection and Other Concentration Zones

Currently, Yangpu's V-Collection and other internet content industry concentration zones mainly provide content creators and their teams with free office space, live streaming rooms and equipment, salon venues, and other physical carriers and facilities. However, resident UP hosts commonly reported that they originally expected V-Collection to embed more soft services based on content creation industry chain characteristics, such as platform order distribution and supply-demand matching services for small and medium UP hosts. But currently, V-Collection does not yet possess business matching functions and upstream-downstream coordination, with quality content industry ecosystem building still needing improvement.

内容提要：评估当前互联网优质内容创作支持政策的有效性，并提出优化建议。

一、主要问题

1、政策执行错位：筛选标准与内容创作主体特征不匹配

通过走访调研内容创作者发现，"浪九条"在杨浦和黄浦两集聚区具体落地执行过程中，存在显著错位现象：一是政策支持对象筛选标准与内容创作主体特征不符。内容创作主体大多以个人或小团队为主，具有小、快、灵特征，大多以适用个人灵活就业形式，或者注册个体工商户形式进行运营和缴纳社保等。但是，杨浦明确规定各UP主及内容创作团队主体若在全区享受"浪九条"支持政策，必须在全区注册公司，以公司形式入驻。

二是政策支持内容与内容创作主体需求不匹配。目前杨浦、黄浦规定可以为满足支持标准的UP主免费提供办公场所，并为其提供上海户口等福利。但是很多UP主反映，内容创作者需要穿行于城市之中进行素材拍摄，对办公场所的依赖度极低。且很多UP主来自上海本市，对上海户口的需求度也并未如其他类型人才那样迫切。

2、政策执行越位：优质内容项目申报存在加码门槛

2025年9月，《上海市互联网优质内容创作类项目申报指南》发布，重点支持高视格品牌活动、优质内容创作、优质内容出海项目。但是，众多内容创作者反映，本次项目申报存在隐形门槛，即若申报者注册地不在杨浦或黄浦区，其提交的内容项目将不被受理。此外，杨浦和黄浦之间并未就优质内容创作领域形成跨区域协同与差异化竞争，两个区目前就互联网内容创作主体资源的争夺日益激烈。

3、政策执行缺位：V集场等集聚区核心功能缺失

当前杨浦V集场等互联网内容产业集聚区，目前主要为内容创作者及其团队提供免费办公场所、直播间及其设备、沙龙场所等物理载体与设施。然而，入驻UP主们普遍反映，原本期待V集场能根据内容创作产业链特征，嵌入更多软性服务，比如：面向小微UP主的平台派单供需对接服务等。但就目前来看，V集场尚不具备业务对接功能以及上下游协同，优质内容产业生态锻造仍然有待完善。
